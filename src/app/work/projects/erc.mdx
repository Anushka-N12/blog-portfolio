## Overview

An autonomous ground vehicle capable of identifying, collecting, sorting & disposing of trash into designated bins. 

## Key Features

 - **Navigation**: Have tried both ROS2 & custom algorithmic approaches.
 - **Sorting**: Trained YOLO model.
- **Hardware**:  Designed budget-friendly solution to carry out task.

## Technologies Used

- **Raspberry Pi / Arduino**: Controls all movement & processing.
- **ROS2 Humble, Turtlebot, Ubuntu, C++**: Logic given to Pi.
